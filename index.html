Welcome to Fintech at UW-Madison!

What We Do

Our mission is to explore the evolving world of fintech by fostering a collaborative community of learners, investors, and innovators. Through hands-on experiences like our stock trading competition, educational discussions, and industry insights, we aim to equip members with the knowledge and skills to navigate and shape the future of financial technology.
